# Welcome to Falkon! 🚀

Thank you for installing the **Falkon Language Support** extension. This welcome guide will help you verify your installation and configure your shortcuts.

## How it works
This extension coordinates with the `falkon` compiler installed on your system:
1. **Syntax highlighting**: Tailors Python-compatible rules for your `.flk` source files.
2. **Build and Run**: Allows you to compile and run your scripts with a single keypress.
3. **Dedicated Terminal**: The extension opens a custom `"Falkon Run"` terminal and runs `falkon build <file>` followed by executing the compiled binary.
